How the Rotation Algorithm Works

The scheduler uses a bucket-draw rotation with forced alternation:

Step 1 — Bucket sorting: Videos split into 4 buckets by [bgType × lyricStyle]: Meme+CAPS, MV+normal, Meme+normal, MV+CAPS. Inside each, videos round-robin across segments and vary by ratio/bg group.

Step 2 — Alternating draw: Draws one from each bucket in rotation: Meme+CAPS → MV+normal → Meme+normal → MV+CAPS. Every consecutive pair differs in BOTH style AND bg type.

Step 3 — Overflow: When MV buckets empty (~450 vs ~920 memes), remaining memes alternate CAPS↔normal.

Step 4 — Swap optimization: 3 passes swapping adjacent pairs to maximize variation score.

Steps 5–6: Round-robin to accounts, then per-account polish passes.

File naming: Each folder's videos grouped in 10s (same background). Position within each 10: 1=Hook normal, 2=Hook CAPS, 3=V1 normal, 4=V1 CAPS, 5=V2 normal, 6=V2 CAPS, 7=V3 normal, 8=V3 CAPS, 9=V4 CAPS, 10=V4 normal.
